## 💡 Travel Tips & Practical Information
1. **Transportation:** Purchase a prepaid Suica or Pasmo card for easy train and bus travel. Consider a Japan Rail Pass if taking multiple intercity trips.
2. **Currency:** Yen (¥). It’s good to carry cash, as some smaller establishments don’t accept credit cards.
3. **Cultural Tips:** Be mindful of quietness on public transport, queue politely, and remove shoes when entering homes or traditional accommodations.
4. **Dining Etiquette:** Avoid walking while eating and use the oshibori (wet towel) provided at restaurants to clean your hands.
5. **Essential Apps:** Download Navitime Japan for navigation and Google Translate for language assistance.
